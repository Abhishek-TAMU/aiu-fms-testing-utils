### Instruction:
Provide a list of instructions for preparing chicken soup for a family of four.

### Response:

To prepare chicken soup for a family of four, follow these steps:

1. Gather the necessary ingredients:
	* 2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breast or thighs
	* 4 cups of chicken broth
	* 1 large onion, diced
	* 3 cloves of garlic, minced
	* 2 carrots, peeled and sliced
	* 2 celery stalks, sliced
	* 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
	* 1 teaspoon of dried basil
	* Salt and pepper, to taste
	* 2 tablespoons of olive oil
2. Chop the vegetables and herbs:
	* Finely chop the onion, garlic, carrots, and celery.
	* Rinse the thyme and basil leaves, and pat them dry with a paper towel.
3. Heat the oil in a large pot:
	* Pour the olive oil into a large pot and place it over medium heat.
4. Sauté the vegetables and herbs:
	* Add the chopped onion, garlic, carrots, and celery to the pot and sauté until they are softened, about 5 minutes.
	* Add the thyme and basil to the pot and sauté for another minute.
5. Add the chicken and broth:
	* Add the chicken to the pot and pour in the chicken broth.
	* Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through.
6. Season the soup:
	* Remove the pot from the heat and stir in salt and pepper to taste.
7. Serve and enjoy:
	* Ladle the soup into bowls and serve hot.

This recipe makes 4 servings. You can also add noodles, rice, or other vegetables to make the soup more hearty. Enjoy!
