### Instruction:
What are the main attractions in Paris ?

### Response:
Ah, Paris, the City of Light! There are so many incredible attractions to explore in this beautiful city, but here are some of the main ones:

1. The Eiffel Tower: This iconic landmark is a must-visit when in Paris. You can climb to the top for breathtaking views of the city.
2. The Louvre Museum: Home to some of the world's most famous artworks, including the Mona Lisa, the Louvre is a must-visit for art lovers.
3. Notre-Dame Cathedral: This stunning cathedral is one of the most famous landmarks in Paris and offers a glimpse into the city's rich history.
4. The Champs-Élysées: This famous avenue is lined with cafes, shops, and theaters, and offers a great spot to people-watch and soak up the Parisian atmosphere.
5. The Arc de Triomphe: This iconic monument honors the soldiers who fought and died for France, and offers panoramic views of the city from its rooftop terrace.

Of course, there are many more attractions to explore in Paris, but these are some of the main ones that you won't want to miss. Have a great trip!
