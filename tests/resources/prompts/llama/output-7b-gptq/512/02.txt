### Instruction:
Explain some popular greetings in Spanish.

### Response:
¡Hola! (OH-lah) - Hello! This is one of the most common greetings in Spanish, used during the day.

¿Cómo estás? (KOH-moh eh-STAH) - How are you? This is another common greeting used to ask about someone's well-being.

¿Qué onda? (KAY OH-nah) - What's up? This is a more casual greeting used with friends or acquaintances.

¿Cómo te va? (KOH-moh eh-VAH) - How are you doing? This is a more formal greeting used in formal situations.
