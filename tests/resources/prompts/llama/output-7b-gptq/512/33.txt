* Biocompatibility: Graphene is highly biocompatible, making it suitable for use in medical applications such as drug delivery and tissue engineering.

* Energy storage: Graphene could be used to create more efficient batteries and supercapacitors, due to its high surface area and conductivity.
* Water purification: Graphene could be used to create highly efficient water purification systems, due to its high surface area and ability to absorb and desorb contaminants.
* Composites: Graphene could be used to create advanced composites that are stronger, lighter, and more durable than traditional materials.
* Electronics: Graphene could be used to create highly efficient and compact electronic devices, due to its high conductivity and small size.
