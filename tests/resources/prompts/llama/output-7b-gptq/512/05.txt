### Instruction:
What are the main operas composed by Gioachino Rossini ?

### Response:
Gioachino Rossini (1792-1868) was a prolific Italian composer who was particularly known for his operas. Some of his most famous operas include:

* The Barber of Seville (Il barbiere di Siviglia) (1812)
* William Tell (Gli amori di William Tell) (1829)
* La Cenerentola (1817)
* The Italian Girl in Algiers (L'italiana in Algeri) (1810)
* The Sea Siren (La sirena del mare) (1823)

These operas are considered some of the best examples of Rossini's work and are still widely performed today.
