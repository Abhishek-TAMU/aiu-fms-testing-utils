### Instruction:
Explain some popular greetings in Spanish.

### Response:
Hola! (Hello!)

In Spanish, there are many ways to greet someone, and here are some popular options:

1. Hola (OH-lah): This is a casual and friendly greeting that can be used at any time of the day.
2. Buenos días (BWEH-nohs DEE-ahs): This means "good morning" and is a more formal greeting that is commonly used until around lunchtime.
3. Buenas tardes (BWEH-nahs TAR-dehs): This means "good afternoon" and is used from around lunchtime until early evening.
4. Buenas noches (BWEH-nahs NOH-chehs): This means "good night" and is used in the evening or when parting ways for the night.
