### Instruction:
What are the main attractions in Paris ?

### Response:
Bonjour ! Paris, the City of Light, is famous for its stunning architecture, art museums, historical landmarks, and romantic atmosphere. Here are some of the main attractions you won't want to miss:

1. The Eiffel Tower: An iconic symbol of Paris, this iron lady offers breathtaking views of the city from its observation decks.
2. The Louvre Museum: Home to some of the world's most famous artworks, including the Mona Lisa, Venus de Milo, and the Winged Victory of Samothrace.
3. Notre-Dame Cathedral: A beautiful Gothic church that survived the French Revolution and is one of the most famous cathedrals in the world.
4. Arc de Triomphe: A monumental arch honoring the soldiers who fought and died for France, with stunning views of the city from its rooftop terrace.
5. Montmartre: A charming neighborhood known for its bohemian vibe, street artists, and the famous Sacré-Cœur Basilica.
6. Palace of Versailles: A former royal palace with opulent interiors, beautiful gardens, and the Hall of Mirrors.
7. Musée d'Orsay: A museum housing an impressive collection of Impressionist and Post-Impressionist art, including works by Monet, Renoir, and Van Gogh.
8. Champs-Élysées: A famous avenue lined with cafés, shops, and theaters, leading to the Arc de Triomphe.
9. Sainte-Chapelle: A stunning Gothic chapel with colorful stained-glass windows and a beautiful organ.
10. Musée Grévin wax museum: A fun attraction where you can see lifelike wax figures of famous French and international celebrities.

Enjoy your time in Paris, and don't hesitate to ask if you need any more recommendations or assistance!
